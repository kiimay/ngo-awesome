Welcome to Awesome Africa Initiative (AAI)

The Awesome Africa Initiatives (AAI) is a Non- Government Organization launched in October 2020, as a membership umbrella organization representing the interest of Community Based Conservation and Tourism organizations in Tanzania. It’s Head Quarters being located in Mang’ula – Morogoro. The NGO is committed to sensitizing and initiating development programs activities that engage, involve and enhancing community’s livelihood through Conservation of Natural resources and their diverse range of requirements. It also deals with restoration of the loss of the rich cultural biodiversity and reduction of livelihood among the communities living around Mang’ula which resulted to immense cultural dilution due to the influx of many tribes that were migrating in the place

Mission and vision

  • Vision

    Our Vision is to become a champion and principal NGO in creating and sustaining long term community empowerment, enhancing and stimulating cultural resources and environmental conservation programs to maximize returns from conservation of natural and cultural resources

ABOUT UMNP

    UMNP(Udzungwa Mauntain National Park) is comprised of five forest reserves, namely; Mwanihana, West Kilombero Scarp, Nanganje, Matundu and Iwonde Forest Reserve around 1950s. It is the only National Park found in the famous Eastern Arc Mountains (a Biodiversity Hotspot) and it covers an estimated area of 1990km square

    The park can be accessed through the following means By road (from Dar-es salaam to Mang’ula 380 KMs ) By air (Kilombero Sugar Airstrip) By railway from Dar es salaam to Kapirimposhi Zambia (TAZARA)
